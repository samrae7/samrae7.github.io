Web Developer

ABOUT ME

A physics graduate with ten years experience working in science communication and web content. I currently work as a Digital Content Developer for the Natural History Museum.

Having caught the web development bug during a part-time course at General Assembly earlier this year, I took a three-month career break in August to do the GA Web Development Immersive course.

Twelve weeks later I'm amazed by how much I've learnt in such a short space of time. I am now looking for a junior web development role so that I can change my career.

PORTFOLIO

New York Times Explorer

Explore 150 years of NYT articles, browsing by keyword, date and location. Built using Angular, Node JS and Express and using the New York Times API.

Demo GitHub

Evolution Game

Take on the role of a bird trying to survive. Unfortunately natural selection starts to get in the way. A simple game built using HTML, CSS, javascript and jQuery..

Demo GitHub

Corals Quiz

A simple interactive quiz, based around the Natural History Museum’s corals exhibition and designed with mobile phones and tablets in mind.
